About the Ceremony

The Brainpals Code Academy Graduation Ceremony 2025 celebrates the achievements of our talented students who have excelled in Software Engineering, Data Science, and Cybersecurity. Join us in honoring their dedication and innovation as they step into the tech world.

Program of the Event

10:00 AM - Opening Ceremony

Welcome address by the Academy Director, followed by a cultural performance.

10:30 AM - Keynote Speech

Inspirational talk by a renowned tech industry leader.

11:00 AM - Award Presentation

Recognizing outstanding students in Software Engineering, Data Science, and Cybersecurity.

12:00 PM - Certificate Ceremony

Graduands receive their certificates, celebrating their achievements.

1:00 PM - Closing Remarks & Reception

Closing speech followed by a networking reception with refreshments.
